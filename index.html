City Liquors

About

The building at 9403 North Nebraska Avenue was built in the early seventies as a bar called Lucky”s. Years later the name was changed to Interstate Lounge, but now City Liquors is the name of the game! For nearly fifty years this has been the spot for good times in the bar and value and selection in the liquor store. Open Monday through Saturday 9AM until 3:00AM and Sunday 11AM until 3:00 AM, we are available for your party needs. The BAR at City Liquors offers pool tables, skill and video games, a DJ Thursday through Saturday nights, and some type of awesome drink special almost every day and night. The liquor store is equipped to serve all your needs with beer, wine, liquor, tobacco, and mixers if you choose to take the party home with you. Come see us soon and remember, Spirits Low?
